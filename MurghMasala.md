# Murgh Masala

## Ingredients
- 4 tomatoes, peeled.
- 100g butter
- 5cm cinnamon stick
- 4 cloves
- 2 onions, finely chopped
- 4 garlic cloves, chopped
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp ground corriander
- 1/4 tsp turmeric
- 1/4 tsp chilli powder
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 500g chicken breast
- 5cm root ginger, grated (finely chopped)
- 1/4 tsp garam masala## 

## Method
1. Blend tomatoes.
2. Melt butter over medium heat, add cinnamon and cloves, fry for 30 seconds until spices are fragrant.
3. Add onions and garlic, fry for 6-8 min (until golden), stirring frequently.
4. Add cumin, corrinder, turmeric, chilli powder and salt and stir for 1 min.
5. Add chicken and fry for 7-8 min until chicken colours.
6. Add tomatoes and 6 tbsp (100ml) boiling aater.
7. simmer for 10 minutes.
8. Add gingr and garam masala and serve.Â
